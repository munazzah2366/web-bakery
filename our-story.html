our story

Built on sisterhood, late-night baking, and a shared love for all things sweet, their bakery is a dream they brought to life together. What makes it special isn’t just the pastries or the recipes—it’s the way everything began in a small kitchen filled with laughter, flour-dusted counters, and the comfort of always having someone there beside you.

Every cake they make carries a memory: of experimenting late into the night, of arguing over tiny details, of tasting something warm straight from the tray and knowing it was made together. One sister brought precision, the other brought instinct, and somehow, between the two, they created something neither could have made alone.

That feeling never left. It’s in every corner of the bakery, in every bite, in the quiet understanding that the best things are never just about what is made—but who it is made with.
